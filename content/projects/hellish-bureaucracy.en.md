Navigate the administrative nightmare of Hell in this darkly humorous tabletop RPG adventure. Face infernal paperwork, demonic contracts, and middle management.

### Challenges

*   **Infernal Paperwork**: Navigate the literal red tape of Hell's administrative system.
*   **Devil's Contracts**: Read the fine print and find loopholes in demonic agreements.
*   **Middle Management**: Deal with bored demons who've been doing this job for millennia.
*   **Office Politics**: Navigate the complex hierarchy of infernal bureaucracy.

### Departments

*   **Soul Processing**: Where new arrivals are sorted and assigned to their eternal departments.
*   **Contract Review**: Legal department handling all mortal-demonic agreements.
*   **Complaints Department**: Where grievances are filed and promptly ignored.
*   **Quality Assurance**: Ensuring all suffering meets Hell's high standards.
