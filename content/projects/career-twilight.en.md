A compact sci-fi horror scenario for use with the Mothership® Sci-Fi Horror Role Playing Game 1E. The crew is sent to evacuate personnel and prisoners from the Twilight, a corporate research vessel.

Something went wrong on **Twilight** long before you arrived. Ambitions, paranoid secrecy, and systemic errors led to a catastrophe. Now the Twilight is a ticking time bomb ready to go off.

…Just another contract. But could this be the twilight of your career?

> **⛔ RESTRICTED — CLEARANCE LEVEL: WARDEN ONLY**
>
> `CASE FILE: TW-CLASS-A // INCIDENT TIMELINE`
>
> **This section contains full plot details and spoilers.**
> If you are a **player**, stop reading now. By continuing you confirm you are the Warden running this session.
>
> * **5 years ago:** The Corporation purchased the tourist vessel *Twilight* and retrofitted it into a research laboratory.
> * **4 years ago:** Dr. Sofia Reiner took charge of the lab and began developing technology to integrate human consciousness into android bodies. Corporate prison inmates were shipped to the *Twilight* for experimentation.
> * **2 years ago:** Having made significant progress, Dr. Reiner secretly began testing the technology on her own staff — systematically murdering scientific personnel and transferring their consciousnesses into androids visually identical to the originals.
> * **1 year ago:** Test subjects began exhibiting increased aggression. Regular doses of specialized tranquilizers and mandatory medical examinations were prescribed.
> * **2 months ago:** Representatives of the Syndicate approached Dr. Reiner, offering to purchase her research.
> * **1 week ago:** Dr. Reiner was caught attempting to leak data to the Syndicate. She was escorted to Corporate HQ along with her closest staff — secretly androids. The remaining personnel were ordered to freeze all research and await further instructions.
> * **4 days ago:** A shortage of tranquilizers caused the android staff's mental state to deteriorate rapidly.
> * **1 day ago:** The Corporation dispatched your crew to evacuate the remaining personnel and prisoners.
> * **1 hour ago:** Dr. Howard Meyer completely lost his mind. He locked himself in the aft section, intent on reaching the reactor and destroying the ship.

## CORPORATE CONTRACT — FORM TW-7 // EVACUATION PROTOCOL

* **CLIENT:** \[REDACTED\] Corporation
* **CONTRACTOR:** \[Your Crew\]
* **VESSEL:** "Twilight" — Inner Rim Sector
* **TYPE:** Personnel Evacuation — Priority Delta

One week ago, Dr. Sofia Reiner — head of the R&D lab aboard the *Twilight* — was caught attempting to transfer classified information to the Syndicate. Dr. Reiner was escorted to HQ along with two top-clearance staff members. The remaining personnel were ordered to freeze operations and await further instructions.

The Corporation has now authorized your squad to complete the evacuation.

**TASK 01 —** Evacuate the employees **(6 people)** and the corporate prisoners **(5 people)**. A crew manifest has been transmitted to each contractor.

**TASK 02 —** Activate the ship's autonomous mode via the internal control panel.

*The Corporation reminds you of your signed NDAs. Exercise extreme caution when transporting prisoners to the corporate vessel's cryopods.*

### COMPENSATION STRUCTURE

* Mission completion — **50,000 cr**
* Per rescued employee (alive) — **+10,000 cr**
* Per live prisoner delivered — **+5,000 cr**
* Corporate lab data secured — **+30,000 cr**

*NDA CLAUSE: Viewing the contents of secured data is strictly prohibited.*
